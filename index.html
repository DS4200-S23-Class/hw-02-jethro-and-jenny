Task Abstraction


Low-level task: Determining the trend of the maximum daily temperature in Vancouver from 1979 to 2021

Medium-level task: Determining the approximate maximum temperature for each month in Vancouver from 1979 to 2021

High-level task: Determining the months in which most airport passengers would fly to/depart from Vancouver

Targets: The Seattle-Tacoma International, Vancouver International, and Portland International Airports (possibly to help people working there determine which days during a year would experience higher/lower trips to/from Vancouver based on the fluctuating temperatures

Low-level task: Comparing the years in which Seattle experienced a daily mean temperature 20 degrees Fahrenheit above normal versus those of Portland

Medium-level task: Counting the total number of days Seattle and Portland experienced daily mean temperatures 20 degrees Fahrenheit above normal each decade

High-level task: Emphasizing the reality of global warming and encouraging people to think of ways to prevent it

Targets: Environmentalists who would benefit with more evidence to support their cause, Americans who live in large cities such as Seattle and Portland who don't care for the environment that much, Anyone who believes that global warming is a hoax
